1. **Take away 1:** Purpose is not a singular discovery, we each have within us unlimited possibilities. We are not one or the other, we are both. We create these false transitions, l am student in visual thinking but an expert at my work, I am this or that. I cannot be everything because I cannot excel in everything. But if I find my purpose then I will love what I am doing and will be happy but while looking for my purpose how not to stagnate but to move freely and fluidly? is by giving room to every possibilities because I am a doctor even when I am a mother and vice versa.
