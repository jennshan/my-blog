3. **Cognitive scripts theory** posits that we have a sense of how things should turn out in a new situation such that we are not overwhelmed by decision making in a day-to-day life. Cognitive scripts are like programmable instructions that we follow in certain situations based on our past experience. We follow it because we feel confident to act in ways that align with societal expectations. If not actively checked they can limit us into thinking only certain career ambitions are possible and can leads into single-minded pursuits. Three cognitive scripts are prescribed in the book,
